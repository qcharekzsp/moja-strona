Pacman Presentation

Pacman is a classic arcade game developed by Namco and first released in 1980. The player controls Pacman, who must eat all the dots inside an enclosed maze while avoiding four colored ghosts. Eating large flashing dots called "Power Pellets" causes the ghosts to turn blue, allowing Pacman to eat them for bonus points.

The game was a massive success and became a cultural icon of the 1980s. It has spawned numerous sequels, merchandise, and even an animated TV series. Pacman is widely regarded as one of the greatest video games of all time and has had a lasting impact on the gaming industry.
